Where do they come from?

This map outlines the countries where the most immigrants came from in 2008.

Which continents are most represented?

Australia gets immigrants from all over the world, however due to geographic proximity and the relative populations of certain continents, we see a much greater number of immigrants from certain parts of the world.

How developed are the nations that immigrants originate from?

Immigrants originate from many nations and may chose to arrive in Australia for many reasons. Typically we hear about those fleeing persecution and seeking a better life, however this isn't always the case. A large portion of Australia's immigrants come from developed nations for the purposes of work, education or seeking a fresh begininning.
